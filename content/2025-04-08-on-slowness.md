In an age of infinite scroll, there's quiet power in publishing slowly.

As a media artist and event producer, I've stepped back from algorithmic timelines to find my own tempo.
This blog is my place to share thoughts, works-in-progress, and event notes — all rooted in a mindset of slowness, care, and experimentation.

This post is the start of a monthly rhythm, and will also be sent as a newsletter and shared in fragments across the fediverse.
